‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I add AI to my group chat?

A: Go to your chat ‚Üí Tap the settings icon ‚Üí "AI Friends" ‚Üí "Add AI Friend". You can choose from community AI agents or create your own custom AI with specific personalities and instructions.

Q: Are my messages private and secure?

A: Yes! Personal AI chats are end-to-end encrypted. Group chats use TLS encryption in transit and secure storage. Voice calls (Vibe Calls) use WebRTC encryption. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: How do I join a chat via invite link?

A: Tap the invite link (https://vibechat-zdok.onrender.com/invite/...) from Messages, Safari, or any app. VibeChat will open automatically and show the chat details. Tap "Join Chat" to become a member.

Q: Can I use AI features for free?

A: Basic AI chat features are free with rate limits. Premium features include unlimited AI conversations, advanced image generation, and custom workflows. Check the app for current pricing.

Q: How do I delete my account?

A: Go to Profile ‚Üí Settings ‚Üí "Delete Account". This permanently removes all your data including messages, media, and personal AI chats. This action cannot be undone.

Q: Why can't I send images or use my camera?

A: Check your permissions: iPhone Settings ‚Üí VibeChat ‚Üí Enable Camera and Photos. You may need to restart the app after granting permissions.

Q: How do Vibe Calls work?

A: Vibe Calls are group voice/video calls. Tap the phone icon in any chat to start or join a call. Calls use WebRTC technology for high-quality, low-latency communication. Premium users get longer call durations.

Q: Can I use translation in real-time?

A: Yes! Tap the translate icon in any chat, select your language, and all messages will be automatically translated. You can toggle translation on/off anytime. Translation works with 100+ languages.

Q: What are Personal AI Chats?

A: Personal AI Chats are private, end-to-end encrypted conversations with AI agents only you can see. Create custom AI assistants for brainstorming, learning, productivity, or just chatting privately.

Q: How do I create custom workflows?

A: Go to Workflows ‚Üí Create Workflow. Set triggers (new message, specific word, schedule, etc.) and actions (AI response, send message, create event). Workflows automate repetitive tasks in your chats.

üîß Troubleshooting

App Issues

‚ö†Ô∏è App crashing or freezing?
1. Force quit the app and reopen
2. Check for updates in the App Store
3. Restart your device
4. Reinstall VibeChat if problem persists

Messages not sending

  • Check your internet connection (WiFi or cellular data)
  • Verify you're still a member of the chat
  • Try sending a message again (swipe to retry failed messages)
  • Check if the chat has been deleted

Not receiving notifications

  • iPhone Settings ‚Üí Notifications ‚Üí VibeChat ‚Üí Ensure notifications are enabled
  • Check Do Not Disturb settings
  • Verify VibeChat has permission in Background App Refresh
  • Log out and log back in to refresh push notification token

Can't hear audio in Vibe Calls

  • Check your device volume is turned up
  • Ensure microphone/speaker are not muted in the call interface
  • Grant microphone permission: Settings ‚Üí VibeChat ‚Üí Microphone
  • Try using headphones or switch between speaker/earpiece
  • Check that another app isn't using the microphone

Translation not working

  • Ensure you have an active internet connection
  • Check that translation is enabled (tap the translate icon)
  • Verify you've selected a target language
  • Some messages in your selected language won't be translated (already correct)

AI not responding

  • Make sure you @mentioned the AI (e.g., @ChatGPT what's the weather?)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Verify the AI hasn't been removed from the chat
  • You may have hit rate limits (upgrade for unlimited)

Image generation failing

  • Check your prompt follows content guidelines (no inappropriate content)
  • Ensure you have sufficient credits/quota
  • Try a simpler prompt if it's very complex
  • Wait a moment and try again (high demand times may have delays)

Universal links not working

  • Ensure VibeChat is installed on your device
  • Try tapping the link from different apps (Messages, Safari, Notes)
  • Long-press the link ‚Üí "Open in VibeChat"
  • Uninstall and reinstall VibeChat to register universal links
