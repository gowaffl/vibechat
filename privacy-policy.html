Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

Your Privacy Matters
VibeChat is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Phone number, name, profile picture, and optional bio
  • User Content: Messages, photos, videos, voice recordings, and AI-generated content you create or share
  • Contacts: With your permission, we access your device contacts to help you find and connect with friends
  • Calendar & Reminders: With your permission, we access your calendar to create and manage event reminders for group chats

1.2 Information Automatically Collected

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, interaction patterns, session duration, and performance metrics
  • Location Data: With your permission, approximate location from photos you share
  • Technical Data: IP address, app version, crash reports, and diagnostic information

1.3 Camera and Media

  • Photos & Videos: Access to capture and share media in chats
  • Photo Library: Access to select and share existing photos from your device
  • Microphone: Access for voice messages and Vibe Calls (voice/video calling)

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Core Functionality

  • Provide and maintain messaging services
  • Enable group chats with AI-powered features
  • Facilitate voice and video calls (Vibe Calls)
  • Deliver AI-generated responses, images, and translations
  • Sync messages across your devices
  • Create calendar events and reminders from chat conversations

2.2 AI Features

  • AI Conversations: Your messages may be processed by AI models (OpenAI GPT, Google Gemini) to generate responses
  • Image Generation: Your prompts are sent to AI services to create custom images
  • Translation: Messages are processed to provide real-time translations
  • Smart Features: TLDR summaries, tone adjustment, context cards, and workflow automation
  • Personal AI Chats: Private conversations with custom AI agents you create

2.3 Improvements & Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve features and user experience
  • Monitor performance and fix technical issues
  • Understand how AI features are used to enhance quality
  • Measure feature adoption and engagement

2.4 Communications

  • Send push notifications for new messages and mentions
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Send important service updates and security alerts

3. Message Encryption & Security

End-to-End Encryption: Your personal AI chat messages are encrypted end-to-end. Only you can read them.

3.1 Group Chat Messages

  • Group chat messages are encrypted in transit using TLS/SSL
  • Messages are stored securely on our servers to enable syncing across devices
  • We implement industry-standard security measures to protect stored messages

3.2 Personal AI Chats

  • Personal chat messages are encrypted end-to-end using AES-256 encryption
  • Encryption keys are stored securely on your device only
  • We cannot read your personal AI chat messages
  • AI processing requires temporary decryption, performed securely on our servers

3.3 Voice Calls (Vibe Calls)

  • Voice and video calls use WebRTC with end-to-end encryption
  • Calls are peer-to-peer when possible; server-relayed when necessary
  • Call metadata (duration, participants) is stored for service quality

4. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to provide VibeChat functionality:

4.1 Infrastructure & Storage

  • Supabase: Database, authentication, and real-time messaging infrastructure
  • Render.com: Backend hosting and API services

4.2 AI Services

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT): Powers AI conversations and text features. Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini: Powers AI image generation. Privacy Policy

4.3 Communications

  • LiveKit: Provides real-time voice and video calling infrastructure. Privacy Policy

4.4 Analytics

  • PostHog: Privacy-focused product analytics to improve the app. Privacy Policy
Important: When you use AI features, your prompts and messages may be sent to third-party AI providers (OpenAI, Google) for processing. These services have their own privacy policies. We only share the minimum data necessary for functionality.

5. Data Sharing & Disclosure

5.1 We DO NOT Sell Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

5.2 When We Share Information

  • With Other Users: Your profile information and messages are visible to chat members
  • With AI Services: Messages and prompts are sent to AI providers for processing features you explicitly use
  • With Service Providers: Trusted partners who help operate our service (hosting, analytics)
  • For Legal Compliance: When required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
  • Business Transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets

6. Data Retention

  • Messages: Retained until you delete them or delete your account
  • Media Files: Stored until deleted or until account deletion
  • Account Information: Retained for the lifetime of your account
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated data retained indefinitely; individual data anonymized after 90 days
  • Backups: May persist in backups for up to 90 days after deletion

When you delete your account, we permanently delete your:

  • Profile information
  • Messages and media
  • Personal AI chats
  • Custom AI agents and workflows

7. Your Rights & Controls

7.1 Access & Control

  • View Your Data: Access your profile and settings in the app
  • Edit Information: Update your name, photo, and bio anytime
  • Delete Messages: Remove individual messages or entire chats
  • Manage Permissions: Control camera, microphone, contacts, and calendar access in device settings

7.2 Privacy Settings

  • Control who can add you to chats
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Disable AI features (translation, image generation) if desired
  • Choose whether to share analytics data

7.3 Data Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

If you're in the EU, UK, or California, you have additional rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Restrict: Request limited processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@vibechat.app

8. Children's Privacy

VibeChat is not intended for children under 13 years of age (or under 16 in the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately and we will delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) for EU data transfers
  • Compliance with Privacy Shield principles where applicable
  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest

10. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we don't sell data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

Categories of Information Collected:

  • Identifiers (name, phone number, device ID)
  • Internet/network activity (usage data, analytics)
  • Geolocation data (approximate location from photos)
  • Audio/visual information (photos, videos, voice messages)
  • User-generated content (messages, AI conversations)

11. Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • TLS/SSL encryption for data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for personal AI chats
  • Secure server infrastructure with regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication systems
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Incident response procedures
Data Breach Notification: In the unlikely event of a data breach affecting your information, we will notify you and relevant authorities as required by law.

12. Cookies & Tracking

Our mobile app uses minimal tracking technologies:

  • Session Management: To keep you logged in
  • Analytics: To understand app usage (via PostHog)
  • Performance Monitoring: To identify and fix issues

We do not use advertising cookies or third-party ad trackers.
